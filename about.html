Who are The Crazy Monkeys?

We are an entirely student run, improvisational comedy group centered at Purdue University. Started in 1996 as an improv group that performed all around the West Lafayette area, we now put on monthly shows in some of the largest venues that Purdue has to offer. All our shows are free of cost and open to anybody who wants to have a laugh. We specialize in short form improv games packed into an hour-long show, which means that our comedy relies heavily on audience participation. Anything can come from the crowd as a suggestion for a scene or game, which makes our shows not exactly PG (or even PG-13 in many cases).
